This blog documents my hobby coding + data analytics projects, mainly focusing on using data science to better understand social and spatial issues. I'm particularly interested in spatial data science, database structures and text mining. I'm currently a student in Quantitative Methods, and I hope to share my learnings and love for data science (esp. for social good!) through this blog.
